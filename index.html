How the Evaluation Works

Before starting the timed session, let's walk through how the interface works.

1
Watch both videos completely
Two videos on the same topic will appear side by side. You must watch both in full — the submit button will remain locked until the combined video time has elapsed. Do not speed up the videos.
2
Read the topic context
Above the videos you'll see the topic, learning objective, and student persona. Use this to understand who the video is made for.
3
Select the better video
Click on a video card or the choice button below to pick the winner. Base your decision on the 4 rubric dimensions you just reviewed.
4
Confirm and continue
Click "Confirm & Next" to submit your choice and load the next pair. You can also skip a pair if you're unsure. The rubric is always available on the evaluation screen.
